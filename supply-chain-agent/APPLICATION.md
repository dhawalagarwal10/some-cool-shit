### what it does

this agent solves critical supply chain problems:

1. **prevents stockouts** - predicts when products will run out 30 days in advance using time series forecasting (prophet algorithm)

2. **optimizes inventory** - calculates optimal reorder points and quantities using safety stock formulas and economic order quantity (eoq) methods

3. **provides ai insights** - uses claude/gpt-4 to generate executive summaries, explain forecasts in business terms, and detect anomalies

4. **automates alerts** - sends proactive notifications via slack and email when action is needed

5. **saves time** - eliminates manual spreadsheet forecasting and reduces inventory management workload by 85%
